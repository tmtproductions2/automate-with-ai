Section 2 – ChatGPT Prompt Library

Copy and paste these prompts into ChatGPT (or other AI language models) and tweak the variables in brackets.

2.1 Blogging & Content Creation

  • Outline Generator: “Create a detailed outline for a blog post about [topic] aimed at [audience], focusing on [key benefit]. Include an introduction, five headings with brief summaries and a conclusion.”
  • Headline Ideas: “Suggest 10 catchy headlines for a blog post about [topic] that will appeal to [audience] and encourage clicks.”
  • Repurposing: “Summarize the key points from this article: [paste article text]. Provide five tweet‑length facts and one LinkedIn post idea.”

2.2 Social Media & Email

  • Email Sequence: “Write a 3‑email welcome sequence for new subscribers to my [business]. The tone should be friendly and authoritative, guiding them toward [call‑to‑action].”
  • Caption Composer: “Generate five engaging captions for an Instagram Reel about [product or service]. Use a conversational tone and include a call‑to‑action to visit [website].”
  • Answering FAQs: “Provide a helpful response to this customer question in fewer than 120 words: [customer question]. Include a friendly greeting and link to [support resource].”

2.3 Product & Offer Creation

  • Digital Product Ideation: “List 10 digital products (ebooks, templates, courses, etc.) that would appeal to [target audience] interested in [topic]. Rank them by potential demand.”
  • Sales Page Copy: “Write persuasive copy for a landing page promoting a [digital product type] called [product name]. Highlight three benefits and include a testimonial from a fictional customer.”
  • Pricing Strategy: “Suggest three pricing tiers for a [digital product type] aimed at [audience]. Explain what features should be included at each tier.”

Section 3 – AI Workflow Templates

These high‑level workflow ideas combine AI with automation tools (e.g., Zapier, Make.com or n8n) to streamline your business. Each step can be implemented using free plans from the respective platforms.

3.1 Automated Content Pipeline

  1. Collect ideas: Maintain a Google Sheet with content topics. Use Zapier to trigger a ChatGPT summary whenever you add a new topic.
  2. Draft generation: Use ChatGPT to generate a blog outline and first draft based on your prompts. Review and edit manually.
  3. Publishing: Connect the Google Doc to your WordPress or Ghost blog via automation to schedule posts.
  4. Repurposing: Trigger a workflow that sends the final blog post to ChatGPT for social media snippets and schedules them via Buffer or Hootsuite.

3.2 Lead Magnet Delivery

  1. Opt‑in collection: Embed a form on your landing page (e.g., using ConvertKit or MailerLite). When someone subscribes, trigger an automation.
  2. File delivery: The automation sends an email with a link to this toolkit and your custom digital products hosted in Google Drive or Dropbox.
  3. Nurture sequence: Follow up with your welcome email sequence generated by ChatGPT to convert subscribers into paying customers.

3.3 Chatbot Support

  1. Build a chatbot: Use ManyChat or Chatfuel to create a chatbot for Facebook Messenger or your website.
  2. Train responses: Feed the bot with your FAQ and knowledge base content. Use AI to draft initial responses and refine manually.
  3. Integrate email collection: Configure the chatbot to collect emails and send them to your mailing list for follow‑up.
